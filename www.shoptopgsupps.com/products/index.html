Neurons in the brain are cells of chemical communication. Human brains are comprised of tens of billions of neurons, each linked to thousands of other neurons. Thus, the brain has a highly specialized network of trillions of connections (synapses). When these neural pathways malfunction or are inhibited, affective (behavioral) symptoms manifest themselves. When compounds, including neuro enhancement nutraceuticals, are able to alter neural function, these behavioral symptoms may be relieved, worsened, or produced. Diminished levels of the brain’s chemical messengers (neurotransmitters) negatively impact the various aspects of attention, such as impulsivity, hyperactivity, deficient arousal, excessive arousal, and stress, which potentially results in a profound impact on cognitive function, particularly memory and mood.

Although some degree of cognitive impairment is inevitable in older years, steps can be taken to prevent, or at least mitigate, the loss of cognitive function. This area of nutraceutical supplementation is highly challenging and is changing rapidly.
